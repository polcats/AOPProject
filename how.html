How to Help?

We, Advocates of Peace, aim to use peace and order in making the world a better place. This aim however will not be possible without your help. Every help counts, so come and join us attain world peace. How to help?

  1. Be athletic. Playing outdoor games is a good exercise for you and reduces the tendency of you being involved in street fights.
  2. Be friendly. Being nice towards other people will help you gain reliable friends and is a great way of avoiding violence.
  3. Work. Working gives you income and keeps you busy, which lessens the possibility that you will get in unwanted situations.
  4. Travel. Explore the beauty of the world and be inspired to promote peace with the hospitality of the Filipinos.
